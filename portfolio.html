pGarfield

Weston Cadena   July 23, 2022

pGarfield is a parallel implementaion of Garfield++, a particle simulation toolkit created by CERN. pGarfield aims to tackle the issue of long simulation times in Garfield++ by utilizing parallel computing techniques. It allows the simulation to run on multiple processors simultaneously, thus reducing the overall simulation time. This makes pGarfield an ideal choice for large-scale simulations that require high computational power. With the power of parallel processing, researchers and scientists can now obtain results faster and make more progress in their research.

Puzzle Solver

Weston Cadena   October 12 2022

Puzzle Solver is a deep learning model designed to solve image puzzles. It works by analyzing an input image that is shuffled and out of order, and then rearranging the pieces to form the correct, completed image. The model is trained on a large dataset of images, allowing it to learn the features and patterns that are unique to different types of puzzles.

FlightGPT

Weston Cadena   January 29 2023

FlightGPT is a user-friendly platform that streamlines the travel planning process. It leverages the advanced capabilities of GPT-3, the latest language model developed by OpenAI, to provide personalized recommendations for travel destinations based on user inputs. The platform allows users to input a prompt about their desired travel location and receive information about popular travel ideas and the best flights to book. The recommendations are generated in real-time, taking into account various factors such as budget, preferred travel dates, and destination preferences.

Minecraft Clayhorses

The Clay Horse mod for Minecraft expands on the popular Clay Soldiers mod by adding a new type of clay creature - horses! These horses are made from clay and can be crafted using clay blocks and various other materials. Once crafted, the horses can be ridden and used for transportation or for battles in the game. Players can also customize their clay horses with different colors and patterns to make them truly unique. With this new addition, players can now experience even more exciting battles and adventures in the world of Minecraft.
