Still Creek Ranch

Mentorship Program

Still Creek Ranch is a boys and girls club near the Bryan area that saves children from crisis situations. At Still Creek Ranch, I serve as a mentor for a boy on the ranch. As I mentor I am a positive male rolemodel as well as a friend for my mentee. With a group, we will go out to the ranch once a week for the mentorship program.

TBarM

Summer Camp Counselor

TBarM is a Christian sports summer camp located in New Braunfels, Texas. The summer of my freshman year, I was a camp counsoler for TBarM. There I would spread the word to younger generations of Christians, and also facilitate fun. I also met other great people there, who were passionate about living life to the fullest.

Refuge

Youth Ministry

Refuge is a youth ministry program that Restoration Church Bryan puts on every Thursday. There, we will pick up the kids from around the neighborhood, and bring them to the church where we will play sports and learn about God. This is a healthy escape for most of the kids involved from there every day life.
